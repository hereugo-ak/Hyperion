Regulatory Analyst

High-Risk Regulation: Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act) 2023 (India)

Source: Unsplash via Katie Moum

Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act) 2023 (India): Regulates the processing of digital personal data in India, including cross-border data flows, consent requirements, and data principal rights.. Risk level: high. Penalty range: INR 50,000 to INR 250 crore (approx. $6,000 to $30 million). Compliance cost: $100,000 - $500,000/year. Goods and Services Tax (GST) Act, 2017 (India): Unified indirect tax system replacing multiple central and state taxes, applicable to the supply of goods and services.. Risk level: high. Penalty range: INR 10,000 to INR 10 lakh (approx. $1,200 to $120,000) or 10% of tax evaded, whichever is higher. Compliance cost: $20,000 - $100,000/year. Factories Act, 1948 (India): Regulates labor conditions, safety, health, and welfare in factories employing 10 or more workers (20 or more with power).. Risk level: high. Penalty range: INR 10,000 to INR 1 lakh (approx. $1,200 to $12,000) or imprisonment up to 2 years. Compliance cost: $30,000 - $150,000/year.
